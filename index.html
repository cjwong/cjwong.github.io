Current Projects

Right now I am focusing on learning about "context effects" or "neighborhood effects" by asking thousands of survey respondents in Canada, the UK, and the USA to hand-draw their "local communities" on Google maps. I am also working on a project aiming to understand public opinion about the granting of citizenship in exchange for service (like military service) in the USA.
